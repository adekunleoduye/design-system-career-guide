Design Systems Career Guide

A book to help you navigate your career in Design Systems

Design systems have been embraced by numerous companies, both big and small. However, the ambiguity surrounding roles presents significant challenges for individuals in this field. In the "Design Systems Career Guide," experts Jina Anne and Adekunle Oduye address these challenges by clarifying roles, providing guidance on career advancement, and recommending best practices for hiring design system candidates. Whether you're an experienced design system practitioner or new, this guide offers invaluable insights and practical advice to help you thrive in this dynamic domain.

Explore the distinct stages of design systems teams' integration within organizational structures.
